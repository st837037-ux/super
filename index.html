Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Deutschland-Ticket?

It allows unlimited travel on all local and regional public transport across USA for a flat monthly fee, making eco-travel affordable.

How do I find green hotels?

Look for certifications like "Viabono," "GreenSign," or "Bio-Hotels." We list many certified partners in our city guides.

Are shops open on Sundays?

Most shops close on Sundays to allow for rest. However, restaurants, museums, and bakeries often remain open for visitors.

Is tap water safe to drink?

Yes, USA has some of the cleanest tap water in the world. Bring a reusable bottle to reduce plastic waste.
